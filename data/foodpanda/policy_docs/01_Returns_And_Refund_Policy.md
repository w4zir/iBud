# Global Returns & Refund Policy
**Objective:** Testing conditional logic based on item category and timeframes.

### Standard Window
* 30 days from the date of delivery.

### Category-Specific Rules
* **Electronics:** 15-day window; must be in original packaging with unbroken seals. A 15% restocking fee applies if opened.
* **Apparel:** 30-day window; tags must be attached. "Final Sale" items are non-returnable.
* **Perishables:** Non-returnable unless arrived damaged.

### Refund Methods
* **Original payment method:** 5–7 business days.
* **Store Credit:** Instant issuance.

### International Orders
* Eligible for refunds but not exchanges.
* Return shipping is at the customer's expense.
