# SwiftShip Logistics & Delays Policy
**Objective:** Testing real-time reasoning regarding shipping tiers and compensation.

### Shipping Tiers
1.  **Standard:** 5–7 business days.
2.  **Expedited:** 2–3 business days.
3.  **Priority Overnight:** Next business day.

### The "Late Arrival" Guarantee
If **Priority** or **Expedited** shipping arrives >24 hours past the estimated window due to carrier error, the shipping fee is 100% refundable.

### Force Majeure Clause
Delays caused by extreme weather, national holidays, or natural disasters do not qualify for shipping refunds.

### Lost Packages
A package is officially "Lost" after 10 business days of no tracking updates. At this point, the agent must offer a free replacement or a full refund.
