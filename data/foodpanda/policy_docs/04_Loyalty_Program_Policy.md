# Elite Tiers Loyalty Program
**Objective:** Mapping customer status to specific benefits.

| Tier | Spend Requirement | Benefits |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bronze** | $0 - $500/year | 5% points back |
| **Silver** | $501 - $2,000/year | 10% points back, Free Standard Shipping |
| **Gold** | $2,001+/year | 15% points back, Free Expedited Shipping, Early access to sales |

### Point Expiration
Points expire if there is no account activity for 12 consecutive months.
