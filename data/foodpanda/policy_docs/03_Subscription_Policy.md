# Subscription & Auto-Renewal Policy
**Objective:** Testing management of recurring billing and pro-rated calculations.

### Cancellation
Users can cancel anytime via the dashboard. If canceled mid-cycle, access remains until the end of the current billing period.

### Refund Rules
No refunds for partial months unless the cancellation request was submitted within **48 hours** of the renewal charge.

### Pausing Subscriptions
Users may pause for up to 3 months. While paused:
* Loyalty discounts are preserved.
* "Premium Perks" are deactivated.
