# Damaged or Incorrect Item Claims
**Objective:** Testing evidence collection protocols.

### Reporting Period
Must be reported within **48 hours** of the "Delivered" timestamp on the tracking page.

### Required Documentation
To process a claim, the customer must provide:
1.  Clear photo of the shipping label.
2.  Clear photo of the external box.
3.  Clear photo of the damaged item.

### Resolution Options
* **Minor Damage:** Offer 20% partial refund to keep the item.
* **Major Damage/Incorrect Item:** Prepaid return label provided + immediate reshipment.
